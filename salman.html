<p class="dropcap">Salman , as a CEO , he oversees the technology strategy and development capacity at SWAP Solutions, keeping it pointed to the top of the pyramid.</p>
<p>Salman has a rich history of building teams and communities at the intersection of technology and business development along side with different fields.He founded CODING101 team at his University which led to revolution in teaching coding coding without computers and computer science in its simplest way. Salman aslo tried to establish Cloud GUC as a community group at the same University .He had a leadding role in creating Google Developer Group at the German University in Cairo . Salman was a founding member of Gernata Academy team where he was responsible for quality assurance.</p>
<p>ElDash saw the need of an entity that believes in the new ideas and help building them , then turning those ideas into masterpiece . His vision escalated to founding SWAP Solution to develop all the ideas out there in the community .</p>
<p>Salman is a Bachelor student at the German University in Cairo, where he is “focusing” on computer science, business development, knowledge analysing , community development and international relations.</p>
