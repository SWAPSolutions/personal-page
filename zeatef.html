Zeyad Atef

Zeyad has only worked in front-end development at SWAP Solutions till now but looking forward to work in back-end development too. Moreover, he was assigned as Share El5eir's frontend team leader.

Zeyad is enthusiastic, meticulous, fast learner and productive. He is up to challenges and loves to work on projects with some risks and a lot of learning. Zeyad takes care of the tiniest details in his work to produce great quality. He can also multitask effectively and is good in prioritizing his work.

In addition to software development, Zeyad is a professional basketball player. He played for a long list of clubs including Alexandria Sporting Club and Al-Ahly Club. He also took part in the Egyptian National Team which participated in the Afrobasket u18 at Maputo, Mozambique.

Zeyad is a Bachelor student at the German University in Cairo, where he concetrates on computer science and software development in parallel with maintaining a professional level in basketball.
